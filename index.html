Welcome to my Repository! Here is where I will post some of my Beta and finished custom Bukkit plugins! You can also find some of my stuff on Bukkit Dev!

About Jedeyeboba

Jedeyeboba is an amateur Plugin developer, but is an experienced Minecraft player. He has his eyes set on creating custom Bukkit plugins that have never been thought of before! Currently, he is creating plugins for his private server. Unfortunately, he is not currently accepting commissions, although that may be an option in the future!

Plugins In Progress

Jedeyeboba is currently working on a new plugin: SherlockIsBored. This plugin, based off of BBC's television show Sherlock, adds the simple ability to be... bored.

Unfortunately due to the recent DMCA takedown of both Bukkit and Spigot, I have ceased programming my plugins. However, a new server program has come to light, and when the Glowstone server platform is completed, I shall continue. Sorry for the inconvenience.
